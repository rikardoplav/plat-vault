Local Market Insights

Sales Price Trend

In May 2026, residential properties for sale in Potenza Picena reached an average asking price of €1,673/m², marking a +14.98% increase compared to May 2025, when the average was €1,455/m². Over the last two years, May 2026 represented the highest recorded average price in the municipality.

Rental Price Trend

In May 2026, residential rental properties in Potenza Picena recorded an average asking rent of €8.82/m², showing a +14.99% increase compared to May 2025, when the average was €7.67/m² per month. Over the last two years, the highest rental value in the municipality was recorded in November 2024, reaching €10.38/m² per month.

International Buyer Demand

The Marche region is showing positive momentum in international real estate demand, with foreign enquiries for homes for sale increasing by +3.32% year over year. This contrasts with the national trend, where international demand decreased by 5.89% over the same annual period. Within the region, the province of Macerata — where Potenza Picena is located — stands out as the strongest performer, accounting for 22.59% of foreign demand in the Marche market.
